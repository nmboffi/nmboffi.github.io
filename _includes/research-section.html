Research Group

The aim of our group is to develop principled methods for flow-based generative modeling and to apply them to problems across science and engineering. We are particularly interested in scientific challenges that are impossible with more traditional computational techniques but which may be possible with machine learning. Current topics of interest include accelerated inference, guidance, and sampling.
